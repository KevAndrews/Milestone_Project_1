Welcome to L.E.A.R.N

Learning English Aligned to Real Needs

L.E.A.R.N Ltd was established in 2014 to help professionals learn English aligned to real needs. Our mission is to create tailored courses matching clients needs to meet tangible language goals. Our team of educational experts have over five years experience teaching English as a Foreign Language and use their experience from degrees and work experience in a range of professional areas including: Business, Law, Marketing, IT and Engineering.

Why study with us?

We provide lessons with industry experts so you can learn English that is best suited to your career needs.

We provide 1 to 1 career interview classes which are designed to help you answer both technical and non-technical questions.

We organise both talks and meet-ups with industry experts to help build connections for your career.

Our campus is perfectly located between 2 major cities so you can practice both Rural and Urban English.
